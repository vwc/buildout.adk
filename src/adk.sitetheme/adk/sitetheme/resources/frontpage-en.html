Augsburg

Enjoy, in one of the oldest cities in Germany, the advantages of a metropolis – pubs, theatres, museums – without getting lost ! Lots of shopping facilities. Learn German in Germany in a friendly, manageable and safe with a student feel in Augsburg.

Why learn German in Germany?

German is the most widely spoken language in Europe. Germany has the 3rd strongest economy and is the number1 export nation in the world. Knowing German creates business opportunities. Germans are the biggest spenders of tourist dollars in the world. German is not as hard as you think. German is required or recommended by many undergraduate and graduate programs.

Accommodation

In Augsburg we offer homestay with friendly German host families near the school. Rooms in shared apartments available (self catered). Stay with a German family and speak German after class! Develop your German language skills effectively!
